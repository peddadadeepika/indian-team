About Indian Team


The India men's national cricket team
represents India in men's international cricket.
It is governed by the Board of Control for Cricket 
in India, and is a Full Member of the International 
Cricket Council with Test, One Day International and
Twenty20 International status. India are the current. 

   The team has played 582 Test matches, winning 180, 
   losing 179, 222 draw and 1 tie. As of August 2024, India 
   is ranked second in the ICC Test Championship on 120 rating
   points. India have played in each World Test Championship  
   final, finishing runners-up in both (2021 and 2023).
